## KEY NOTES

1. **English-speaking country** — ALL university programs in Malta are taught in English. No need to learn Maltese/Italian
2. **EU/EEA students get FREE bachelor's** at University of Malta — government-funded
3. **Small country advantage** — easy to navigate, close-knit international community, safe
4. **Schengen member** — student visa allows travel to all 29 Schengen countries
5. **Mediterranean climate** — warm weather year-round (unlike Northern Europe)
6. **Working rights** — students can work 20 hours/week (with work permit from Identity Malta)
7. **Post-study work** — Malta offers post-graduation work opportunities, especially in IT, iGaming, and finance sectors
8. **MCAST** is Malta's main vocational college — offers degrees at lower cost than UM
9. **QMUL Malta** (Barts London) offers a **full UK medical degree (MBBS)** on Gozo island
10. **Digital Nomad Visa** — Malta also popular for remote workers/digital nomads
