## GENERAL NOTES

1. **EU/EEA Students: Tuition is FREE** at all Swedish universities (public AND private)
2. **Non-EU/EEA Students: Must pay tuition** — SEK 80,000–295,000/year depending on program
3. **Application Fee:** SEK 900 (~€79 / ~৳10,080) for non-EU/EEA students
4. **Currency:** 1 SEK ≈ 0.088 EUR ≈ 11.2 BDT (April 2026)
5. **Central Application:** All applications go through https://www.universityadmissions.se
6. **Deadline:** Autumn intake: apply by January 15. Spring intake: apply by August 15.
7. **Scholarships:** Swedish Institute Scholarships (SISS) for certain countries; many universities offer tuition fee waivers
8. **Accepted IELTS Types:** ONLY Academic, Online, UKVI Academic. General Training NOT accepted.
9. **Accepted TOEFL Types:** ONLY iBT, iBT Home Edition, iBT Paper Edition. ITP/MyBest/Essentials NOT accepted.
