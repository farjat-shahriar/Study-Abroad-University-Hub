## KEY NOTES

1. **EU/EEA students get completely FREE education** — no tuition at any Danish institution
2. **8 research universities** — all world-class, 5 ranked in QS top 500
3. **Danish Government Scholarships (DGS)** — available for BD, PK, IN students (tuition waiver + monthly stipend)
4. **PhD students are EMPLOYEES** — earning ~€3,750–€4,560/month with full benefits (best in Europe)
5. **700+ English-taught programs** — Denmark is one of the most English-friendly countries in Europe
6. **SU grant** — Danish/EU students also receive SU (Statens Uddannelsesstøtte) government study grant of ~DKK 6,500/month
7. **Centralized application** — Bachelor's via optagelse.dk (deadline March 15). Master's direct to university (Jan–March)
8. **CPR number** — all students get a CPR (civil registration number) giving access to free healthcare
9. **Working rights** — 20 hours/week during semester, full-time June–August
10. **Post-study work** — Denmark offers Establishment Card (6-month job search visa after graduation)
