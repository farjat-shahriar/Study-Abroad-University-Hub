## GENERAL NOTES

1. **EU/EEA/Swiss students pay only €178–€628/yr** — one of the cheapest in Europe
2. **Non-EU students pay €2,895 (BSc) or €3,941 (MSc)/yr** — still very affordable compared to UK/US/Australia
3. **Over 60% of French public universities opted OUT of non-EU differentiated fees** — many still charge EU rates to everyone
4. **Doctorate: €397/yr for ALL students** (no differentiated fee)
5. **CVEC: €105/yr** mandatory contribution for all students (Contribution Vie Étudiante et de Campus)
6. **Campus France "Études en France"** is mandatory for students from ~40 countries including Bangladesh, Pakistan, India
7. **1,600+ English-taught programs** available across French universities
8. **CAF Housing Aid**: International students can apply for French government housing subsidies (€100–€300/month)
9. **Scholarships**: Eiffel Excellence Scholarship (Master/PhD), Campus France scholarships, university-specific grants
