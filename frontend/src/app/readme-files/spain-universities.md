## KEY NOTES

1. **Spain has NO central application system** — apply directly to each university (unlike Sweden/France)
2. **Credential recognition (Homologación)** is mandatory for non-EU students — takes 2-6 months
3. **PCE exam** (via UNED) is needed for competitive bachelor's programs — tests specific subjects
4. **Fees vary by region** — Catalonia most expensive, Andalusia/Galicia cheapest
5. **Non-EU students may pay higher fees** than EU students, especially in Catalonia and Madrid
6. **Spanish Bachelor's = 4 years (240 ECTS)** — longer than 3-year Bologna Bachelor's in other EU countries
7. **Working rights** — students can work 20 hours/week alongside studies
8. **NIE number** required for all foreign students living in Spain
9. **Scholarships** — Spanish government MECD scholarships, university-specific grants, Erasmus Mundus
