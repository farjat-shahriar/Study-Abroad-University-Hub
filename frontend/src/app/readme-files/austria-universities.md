## TUITION FEES OVERVIEW (2025-2026)

### Public Universities (Offentliche Universitaten)
| Category | Per Semester | Per Year | Notes |
|----------|------------|----------|-------|
| EU/EEA/Swiss citizens | EUR 0 (tuition-free) | EUR 0 | Only OH fee of EUR 25.20/semester applies |
| Non-EU/EEA students | EUR 726.72 | EUR 1,453.44 | Plus OH fee of EUR 25.20/semester |
| OH fee (all students) | EUR 25.20 | EUR 50.40 | Austrian Students' Union membership + insurance |

> **Note:** Non-EU citizens from "Least Developed Countries" (per OECD DAC list) are exempt from tuition fees. Bangladesh is on this list, so Bangladeshi students may be exempt. Pakistan and India are NOT on this list.

### Private Universities (Privatuniversitaten)
- Fees vary significantly: EUR 3,000 - EUR 35,000+ per year
- No distinction between EU and non-EU fees at most private universities
- Each institution sets its own fees (see individual entries below)

### Universities of Applied Sciences (Fachhochschulen)
- Some charge the same as public universities (EUR 363.36/semester for EU, EUR 726.72/semester for non-EU)
- Others (especially private FHs) charge significantly higher fees (up to EUR 15,000/semester)
- Each FH sets its own fee structure (see individual entries below)

## GENERAL ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

### Bachelor's Programs (International Students)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Secondary school certificate | Must be equivalent to Austrian Matura (Reifeprufung). HSC/A-levels/12th grade completion required |
| German language proficiency | OSD B2 or C1 / TestDaF 4x4 / Goethe-Zertifikat B2/C1 (for German-taught programs) |
| English language proficiency | IELTS 6.5+ / TOEFL iBT 80-88+ (for English-taught programs; University of Vienna requires IELTS 8.0 / TOEFL 115 for some programs) |
| University entrance qualification | Proof that you are eligible for university admission in your home country |
| Supplementary exams | May be required if your school-leaving certificate is not fully equivalent to Austrian Matura |
| Application deadline (Winter) | Non-EU: by 1 August; EU: by 5 September |
| Application deadline (Summer) | Non-EU: by 8 January; EU: by 5 February |

### Master's Programs (International Students)

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Bachelor's degree | Minimum 180 ECTS (at least 150 ECTS completed for conditional admission) |
| Transcripts with grades | Official transcripts with ECTS/credit hours, legalized and sworn-translated if not in German or English |
| German language proficiency | OSD C1 / TestDaF 4x4 / Goethe-Zertifikat C1 (for German-taught programs) |
| English language proficiency | IELTS 6.5-7.0 / TOEFL iBT 88-100+ (for English-taught programs) |
| Relevant bachelor's degree | Must be in the same or closely related field |
| Motivation letter & CV | Required by most programs |

### Accepted German Language Certificates
- OSD Zertifikat (B2 or C1 depending on program)
- TestDaF (level 4 in all sections)
- Goethe-Zertifikat (B2 or C1)
- DSH (level 2 or 3)
- telc Deutsch (B2 or C1 Hochschule)

### Accepted English Language Certificates
- IELTS Academic (typically 6.5+, some programs require 7.0 or 8.0)
- TOEFL iBT (typically 80-100+)
- Cambridge C1 Advanced / C2 Proficiency
- Pearson PTE Academic (typically 59+)

## COUNTRY-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS: BANGLADESH, PAKISTAN, INDIA

### Competence Check (Mandatory)

Students who completed their previous education in **Bangladesh, Pakistan, or India** must undergo a **mandatory Competence Check at VFS Global** before applying to any Austrian university.

| Detail | Information |
|--------|-------------|
| Where | VFS Global centers in Bangladesh, Pakistan, or India |
| Fee | Approximately EUR 100 (paid directly to VFS Global) |
| When to book | Before submitting university application |
| What to specify | Target Austrian university and desired degree program |
| What it checks | Verification of academic credentials and qualifications |
| Does NOT replace | Formal admission review, selection procedures, or supplementary exams |

### Bangladesh-Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Bachelor's | Master's |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| Academic qualification | HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) with minimum GPA | Bachelor's degree (4 years, minimum 180 ECTS equivalent) |
| Tuition fee exemption | Bangladeshi students may qualify for tuition fee exemption (Bangladesh is on OECD DAC "Least Developed Countries" list) | Same exemption may apply |
| Document legalization | Documents must be legalized by Bangladesh Ministry of Education + Austrian Embassy | Same |
| Competence Check | Mandatory via VFS Global | Mandatory via VFS Global |
| Student visa | Visa D required for studies longer than 6 months | Same |
| Financial proof | Approx. EUR 12,000/year in blocked account or scholarship proof | Same |
| Health insurance | Mandatory health insurance required | Same |

### Pakistan-Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Bachelor's | Master's |
|-------------|-----------|----------|

| CGPA requirement | Minimum CGPA varies by university (typically 2.5-3.0 on 4.0 scale) | Minimum CGPA varies by university |
| Tuition fees | EUR 726.72/semester at public universities (NOT exempt) | Same |
| Document legalization | HEC attestation + legalization by Pakistani MoFA + Austrian Embassy | Same |
| Competence Check | Mandatory via VFS Global | Mandatory via VFS Global |
| Student visa | Visa D required | Same |

### India-Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Bachelor's | Master's |
|-------------|-----------|----------|

### Visa Requirements (All Three Countries)

| Step | Details |
|------|---------|
| 1. Admission letter | Obtain admission from an Austrian university |
| 2. Competence Check | Complete VFS Global competence check (EUR ~100) |
| 3. Financial proof | Blocked account with EUR ~12,000/year OR scholarship letter |
| 4. Health insurance | Valid health insurance coverage for Austria |
| 5. Visa D application | Apply at Austrian Embassy/Consulate in home country |
| 6. Residence permit | Apply for "Aufenthaltsbewilligung Student" after arrival in Austria |
| 7. Registration | Register at university within admission deadline |
