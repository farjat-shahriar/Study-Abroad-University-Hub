### B. COUNTRY-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

#### Bangladesh

##### Bachelor's

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Accepted Qualification** | Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) / Alim Certificate |
| **Equivalency** | HSC is generally accepted as equivalent to US high school diploma. Evaluated by WES (World Education Services) for official equivalency |
| **WES Evaluation** | Strongly recommended — costs ~$100–$220. Many US universities require WES or NACES-member evaluation |
| **GPA Conversion** | Bangladeshi GPA 5.0 scale → US 4.0 scale (approximately: 5.0 BD = 4.0 US; 4.0 BD = 3.2 US) |
| **English** | HSC English NOT accepted for US admissions. Must submit IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 80+ |
| **SAT/ACT** | Recommended for merit scholarships at state universities; some schools test-optional |
| **Financial Proof (F-1 Visa)** | Bank statement or sponsor letter showing $50,000–$70,000/yr in available funds |
| **F-1 Visa** | Required. Apply after receiving I-20 from university. SEVIS fee $350 |

##### Master's

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Accepted Degree** | 4-year Bachelor's (BSc/BA/BBA Honours) from a recognized Bangladeshi university |
| **3-Year Degrees** | 3-year pass course degrees may NOT be accepted at top schools. Honours degree strongly preferred |
| **WES Evaluation** | Required by most programs. Comprehensive evaluation ($220) recommended |
| **GPA** | Minimum 3.0/4.0 equivalent (approx. 3.0/4.0 on BD scale or 60%+ marks) |
| **English** | IELTS 6.5–7.0 or TOEFL 80–100 (program dependent) |
| **GRE** | Required/recommended at many STEM and sciences programs |

#### Pakistan

##### Bachelor's

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Accepted Qualification** | Higher Secondary School Certificate (HSSC) / Intermediate Certificate |
| **IBCC Attestation** | IBCC attestation is NOT required by US universities (it is for Sweden/Europe). US schools accept HSSC directly |
| **WES Evaluation** | Required by most universities — HSSC evaluation through WES or NACES member |
| **GPA Conversion** | Pakistani percentage: 90%+ = 4.0 GPA; 80–89% = 3.5–3.9; 70–79% = 3.0–3.4 |
| **English** | HSSC English NOT accepted. Must submit IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 80+ |
| **SAT/ACT** | Recommended for merit scholarships; many schools test-optional |
| **Financial Proof (F-1 Visa)** | Bank statement showing $50,000–$70,000/yr available. Sponsor letter acceptable |

##### Master's

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **Accepted Degree** | 4-year Bachelor's from HEC-recognized Pakistani university |
| **16-Year Education** | US graduate schools require minimum 16 years of education (Matric 10yr + Inter 12yr + BSc 4yr). 14-year pass degrees (Matric + FA + BA 2-yr) generally NOT accepted without a bridging year |
| **WES Evaluation** | Required. Comprehensive evaluation for equivalency |
| **GPA** | Minimum 3.0/4.0 equivalent (approx. 60%+ marks or CGPA 3.0/4.0) |

## GENERAL NOTES

1. **Tuition Figures:** All tuition figures are approximate for the 2025-2026 academic year. Costs increase by 2–5% annually. Always check the official financial aid office of each university.
2. **International = Out-of-State:** International students always pay out-of-state tuition rates regardless of where they live or how long they have been in the US.
3. **Total Cost of Attendance:** Add $12,000–$22,000/yr for room, board, books, and personal expenses on top of tuition, making total annual costs $35,000–$70,000 at most major public universities.
4. **Currency:** 1 USD ≈ 110 BDT ≈ 278 PKR ≈ 84 INR (April 2026)
5. **F-1 Student Visa:** Required for all international students. Must be enrolled full-time (min. 12 credit hours for UG; 9 for grad). OPT available after graduation (12 months + 24-month STEM extension).
6. **Common App:** Most public universities accept the Common App (https://www.commonapp.org). Some large state schools (UT Austin, UC system) use their own application systems.
7. **UC Application System:** The University of California system uses its own portal at https://apply.universityofcalifornia.edu. You can apply to multiple UC campuses in one application. Deadline: November 30.
8. **QS Rankings:** Rankings shown are QS World University Rankings 2026. US News Rankings and THE Rankings may differ.
9. **Test-Optional Policies:** Many US public universities became test-optional during COVID-19 and have extended or made permanent these policies. SAT/ACT scores can still strengthen applications for merit scholarships.
10. **WES Credential Evaluation:** World Education Services (https://www.wes.org) evaluations are required or strongly recommended by most US graduate programs for applicants from BD, PK, IN, and other countries.
