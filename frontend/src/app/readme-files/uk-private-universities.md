## ACCREDITATION & QUALITY ASSURANCE

| Body | Role |
|------|------|
| **OfS (Office for Students)** | Registers and regulates higher education providers in England |
| **QAA (Quality Assurance Agency)** | Reviews academic quality of UK universities and colleges |
| **HESA** | Collects and publishes data on UK higher education |
| **Degree Awarding Powers (DAPs)** | Universities must hold DAPs from OfS — verify before enrolling |
| **ATAS (Academic Technology Approval Scheme)** | Required for certain postgraduate science/engineering subjects for non-EU students |

> **Critical**: Always verify that the institution holds **degree-awarding powers** from OfS (England) or equivalent body. Some private colleges offer degrees awarded by a partner public university — the awarding institution appears on your degree certificate.

## GENERAL NOTES

1. **Degree-Awarding Powers**: Not all private colleges award their own degrees — many partner with a public university (e.g., University of Hertfordshire, Middlesex University) to award degrees. Check which university name appears on your degree certificate.
2. **BPP University**: One of the UK's largest private universities — strong in Business, Law, and Accountancy. Professional focus with CFA, ACCA, and Bar qualification links.
3. **The University of Law (ULaw)**: UK's largest and most established law school — has 14 campuses across the UK. Offers LLB, GDL (Graduate Diploma in Law), LPC, and Bar practice courses. Degrees are recognized by the Solicitors Regulation Authority and Bar Standards Board.
4. **Regent's University London**: Only private university in Regent's Park — known for small class sizes, international student body (150+ nationalities), and Liberal Arts/Business focus.
5. **Northeastern University London**: US university's London campus — offers degrees validated by Northeastern University (USA). Strong tech and computer science programs.
6. **Post-Study Work Visa**: All students completing a degree at a registered UK higher education provider (public or private) are eligible for the 2-year Graduate Route visa — including private university graduates.
7. **Foundation Programs**: Most private UK universities offer 1-year foundation programs (NFQ Level 3 equivalent) for international students who need academic preparation before degree entry. These are excellent pathways for students with HSC, FSc, or Class 12 who don't meet direct entry.
8. **Online Learning**: Arden University specializes in online and blended learning — popular with working professionals seeking UK qualifications with flexible study.
9. **London Business School (LBS)**: While classified under University of London's umbrella, LBS functions essentially as a private graduate business school. It consistently ranks #1–5 globally for MBA and is extremely selective (GMAT 700+ median).
10. **BIMM and SAE**: These specialist music/creative institutions are fully accredited — BIMM is degree-awarding; SAE partners with public universities for degree validation. Both have strong industry links with the music, film, and gaming industries.
