## KEY NOTES

1. **Tuition Fees**: Most public universities in Germany do NOT charge tuition fees for domestic and international students. Only Baden-Württemberg charges €1,500/semester for non-EU international students.
2. **Semester Contribution**: All students pay a semester contribution (Semesterbeitrag) of approximately €100-€400 per semester, which covers student services, public transportation tickets, etc.
3. **DHBW Campuses**: The Duale Hochschule Baden-Württemberg (DHBW) operates across 12 locations: Stuttgart, Mannheim, Karlsruhe, Lörrach, Mosbach, Ravensburg, Heidenheim, Villingen-Schwenningen, Heilbronn, Friedrichshafen, and more.
4. **Bundeswehr Universities**: Germany has 2 military universities (Helmut Schmidt University in Hamburg and Universität der Bundeswehr München) which are public but primarily serve military personnel.
5. **Language**: Many programs at public universities are taught in German. However, an increasing number of Master's programs (and some Bachelor's programs) are offered in English.
