## KEY NOTES

1. **Stipendium Hungaricum** is Hungary's biggest advantage — fully funded scholarship for 80+ countries including BD, PK, IN
2. **Medical programs in English** — Hungary is one of the top destinations for international medical students in Europe
3. **NO IELTS required** at many universities — MOI (Medium of Instruction) certificate is widely accepted
4. **Lower IELTS requirements** than most EU countries — IELTS 5.5 for bachelor (vs 6.0-6.5 in Sweden/France)
5. **Affordable tuition** — €2,500–€7,000/yr is much cheaper than UK, Netherlands, or Scandinavia
6. **Affordable living** — Budapest living costs ~€500–€800/month (cheaper than Western Europe)
7. **Working rights** — students can work 24 hours/week during semester, full-time in breaks
8. **EU degree** — all Hungarian degrees are recognized across the EU under the Bologna Process
9. **English-taught programs** — 500+ programs available in English across all fields
10. **Schengen visa** — student visa allows travel to all 29 Schengen countries
