### C. COUNTRY-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

#### Bangladesh

| Level | Requirements |
|-------|-------------|
| **Bachelor** | HSC (Higher Secondary Certificate) with minimum GPA 3.5/5.0 (private colleges are more flexible than public universities). IELTS 5.5–6.0 (no band below 5.0). Apply directly to each college's international office |
| **Master** | Bachelor's degree (3-year or 4-year) from a UGC-approved Bangladeshi university. CGPA 2.5/4.0+ acceptable at most private colleges. IELTS 6.0–6.5 |
| **Documents** | HSC certificate + mark sheets, SSC certificate, National ID + passport, financial documents, English proficiency certificate, 2 reference letters |
| **Visa** | Irish Study Visa (C visa or D visa for stays over 90 days). Must show €10,000+ in bank for visa application |
| **Conditional Offers** | Private colleges commonly issue conditional offers — English proficiency and document submission can be completed after receiving offer |
| **Work Rights** | 20 hrs/week during term, 40 hrs/week during June–September holidays |
| **Extra Notes** | Private colleges like GCD, DBS, NCI have dedicated Bangladesh student communities and support services |

#### Pakistan

## ACCREDITATION & QUALITY ASSURANCE

| Body | Role |
|------|------|
| **QQI (Quality and Qualifications Ireland)** | Validates all Irish qualifications on the NFQ (National Framework of Qualifications) |
| **HEA (Higher Education Authority)** | Oversees publicly funded HEIs; some private colleges are also HEA-designated |
| **HETAC (historic)** | Now merged into QQI |
| **Professional Bodies** | ACCA, CPA, CIMA (for accounting); IMC (marketing); Law Society (law) |

> **Important:** Always verify that the specific program is **QQI-accredited** before enrolling. QQI accreditation ensures your degree is recognized in Ireland and across the EU.

## GENERAL NOTES

1. **QQI Accreditation is critical**: All degree programs at private Irish colleges must be QQI-validated to be recognized in Ireland and EU. Verify before enrolling.
2. **RCSI** is technically an independent (private-aided) institution but is state-aided and one of the most prestigious medical schools in the English-speaking world with campuses in Bahrain, Dubai, Malaysia, and Rwanda.
3. **Griffith College Dublin (GCD)**: Largest independent (private) college in Ireland — offers degrees in Law, Business, Journalism, Computing, and Music. Its law degrees are recognized by the Law Society of Ireland.
4. **NCI IFSC Location**: National College of Ireland is uniquely located in Dublin's Financial Services Centre — ideal for Finance, FinTech, and Data Analytics students.
5. **Post-Study Work**: Same as public universities — eligible for 1–2 year Stay Back Permission after completing a QQI Level 8 or higher qualification.
6. **Work Rights**: Same as public university students — 20 hrs/week during term, 40 hrs/week during holidays.
7. **Foundation Programs**: Most private colleges offer 1-year foundation programs for international students who do not meet direct entry requirements.
8. **Pathway to Public Universities**: Some private college programs have articulation agreements with TU Dublin and other public universities — students can transfer after 1–2 years.
