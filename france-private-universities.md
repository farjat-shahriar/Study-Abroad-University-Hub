## KEY NOTES

1. **France is one of the cheapest countries for international students** — public university fees are only €2,895/yr (BSc) and €3,941/yr (MSc) for non-EU students
2. **Grandes Écoles** are elite institutions with selective admission — different from regular universities
3. **Business school fees** are much higher (€10,000–€50,000+) but include internships, career services, global networks
4. **Engineering schools** are surprisingly affordable (€628–€3,941 for public ones)
5. **Campus France procedure** is mandatory for students from ~40 countries before visa application
6. **CAF Housing Aid** — even international students can receive French government rent subsidies (€100–€300/month)
7. **Eiffel Scholarship** — covers tuition + living costs for Master's and PhD students
8. **Working rights** — students can work 964 hours/year (20 hours/week) alongside studies
